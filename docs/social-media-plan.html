Goal · Brand Awareness

1. Goal & approach

The objective is brand awareness: make Juba Consultants recognised across South Africa as the integrated Skills Development · AI · ICT brand.

  • All four platforms stay active, each with a distinct role (section 4).
  • Operating model: plan once a month, post once a week — one "hero" post per week, repurposed across all four platforms (section 6).
  • Consistency over volume: the same look, voice and weekly rhythm build recognition faster than sporadic bursts.

6. Operating model: plan monthly, post weekly

Your rhythm: a short monthly planning session, then one post per week — published and repurposed across all four platforms.

① Monthly planning session — once a month (~1–2 hours)

  • Pick the month's theme and the 4 weekly posts (one per week), rotating across pillars.
  • Batch-create all four assets in one sitting (shoot/design together).
  • Write the four captions and schedule them (Meta Business Suite / Buffer).
  • Refresh the idea backlog for next month.

② Weekly post — once a week

  • One "hero" post goes out each week, adapted to each platform (section 9).
  • Optional: light stories / behind-the-scenes (#ThroughTheLens) and ~10 minutes of daily engagement.

9. Work smart: create once, post everywhere

One weekly post should reach all four platforms — adapt, don't recreate.

  • A video post → IG Reel + TikTok + Facebook (+ LinkedIn if relevant) — trim captions/hashtags per platform.
  • A graphic / carousel → Instagram + LinkedIn (as a document) + Facebook.
  • A written / thought piece → LinkedIn-native, shortened for Facebook, and turned into an on-brand quote graphic for Instagram.

Create all four of the month's posts in your monthly planning session, then schedule them with a free tool (Meta Business Suite for IG + FB; Buffer for all four).

10. Measuring awareness

Review monthly. For awareness, watch reach and growth more than likes.

Reach / impressionsHow many people saw you — the core awareness number.

Engagement rateQuality of connection (likes + comments + shares ÷ reach).
Shares & savesStrongest awareness signal — others spreading your content.

Profile visits & website clicksIntent — people going deeper.

11. Quick-start checklist

  • Secure consistent handles & identical bios across IG, FB, TikTok, LinkedIn (logo profile pic, gold accent).
  • Add a "link in bio" (website + key pages) via a simple link hub.
  • Build 3–4 branded post templates (quote, tip, spotlight, event).
  • Connect Instagram + Facebook in Meta Business Suite for scheduling.
  • Hold the monthly planning session (1–2 hrs): set the theme, plan the 4 weekly posts, batch-create the assets and captions, and schedule them.
  • Post once a week; repurpose each post across all four platforms.
  • Engage ~10 minutes daily: reply to comments, follow & comment on partners (SETAs, Moses Kotane, local orgs).
  • Review metrics at month-end (in the next planning session); double down on what reached most.
