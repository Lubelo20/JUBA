## 3. What the redesign delivers

The redesign keeps **all of the above** but presents it through a modern,
professional, fast and mobile-first experience.

### Design direction — "Modern Minimal"
- Clean, confident layout with generous spacing and clear hierarchy.
- **Brand palette:** Navy `#0B2E6D`, Navy-dark `#071d45`, Gold `#C9A34E`.
- **Typography:** Poppins — modern, friendly, highly legible.
- Consistent, reusable component system (buttons, cards, section headers, eyebrow
  labels) so every page feels like one product.

### What is already built in the mockup
| Page | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Hero → about teaser → services preview → "Who We Serve" → call-to-action |
| **About** | Company story, values, founder card |
| **Services** | Full services grid |
| **Gallery** | Photo grid with click-to-zoom lightbox + video section (with poster frames) |
| **Contact** | Contact details + working enquiry form |

### Improvements over the current site
- **Modern, cohesive visual identity** — a single design system instead of mixed styles.
- **Mobile-first & fully responsive** — adapts cleanly from desktop → tablet → phone
  (3 → 2 → 1 column layouts).
- **New media Gallery** — showcases real workshops and training: an 8-image grid
  with an accessible lightbox, plus 8 short videos that load only when played
  (keeps the page fast).
- **Performance** — no heavy frameworks; images lazy-load; videos use lightweight
  poster frames and do not download until pressed.
- **Accessibility** — keyboard-navigable, visible focus states, descriptive alt
  text and ARIA labels, screen-reader-friendly dialog for the gallery.
- **Working enquiry form** — submissions handled and validated, ready to wire to
  Juba's inbox.
- **Maintainable foundation** — clean HTML/CSS/JS that any developer can extend.

### Content from the current site → where it lives in the redesign

| Skills Development & Training | Services / dedicated division page |
| AI Breakthrough | **New** division page *(to be added in final phase)* |

## 4. This is a mockup — what that means

The prototype exists to **agree the design and structure before we invest in the
final build**. In the mockup:

- Photos and videos are **placeholders** (taken from recent sessions on a phone).
- Some copy is **drafted / illustrative**, not final approved wording.
- Contact details and social links use **placeholder values** pending confirmation.
- The AI / ICT / Incubation sections are **outlined but not yet built out**.

None of this is a limitation of the approach — it is the normal prototype stage.
Once you approve direction, we replace placeholders with the real thing.
